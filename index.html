Background

Belly Buttons of 661 Test subjects were swabbed to characterize each subject's belly button microbiome. Bacterial DNA is extracted from the samples and 16S ribosomal RNA gene is PCR amplified. Then the resulting amplicons were sequenced using NGS and taxonomy is assigned based on sequence similarity of 97%, resulting in Operational Taxonomic Units (OTU). The resulting sequences were tallied for the relative abundance of Bacterial species within the sample.
